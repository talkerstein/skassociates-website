What we actually do

We run your back-of-house like it's ours.

Most processors sell you a terminal and disappear. We're not most processors. We're a Clover Authorized Dealer with in-house support and an in-house integration team — which means the person who picks up your call is also the person who can fix the problem. We empower our team to do that without permission slips.

01

Authorized, not authorized-adjacent

We're a Clover Authorized Dealer — direct line, direct support, real authority to provision, configure, and fix devices. Not a reseller pretending to be one.

In-house support, in-house engineering

Your support rep is on our payroll, in our office, sitting two desks from our engineering team. When the integration breaks, the people who built it are the people who fix it.

We give our team the authority and the budget to fix problems on the first call. No three-tier escalation. No "let me check with my manager." If something's broken, it gets unbroken.

EN or FR, first language. From the first call to the integration docs to month-36 support. Quebec-built means Quebec-fluent — not translated, not routed offshore.
