Pricing & fees

The most-asked questions. We don't post a rate sheet because rates depend on your card mix and channel — but we tell you exactly how pricing works, and what fees you won't pay.

What's my actual rate going to be?

Your effective rate depends on your card mix (debit vs credit vs premium), average ticket, monthly volume, and channel (in-person, online, or both). For a typical small restaurant or retail merchant, blended effective rates land between 1.9% and 2.6%.

The only way to give you a real number is to read your current statement. Send us yours and we'll come back with a precise quote within 24 hours.

Is it flat-rate or interchange-plus? Which is better?

We offer both. Interchange-plus is more transparent — you see the actual card network cost separately from our markup. Flat-rate is simpler — one number, one rate, no math.

For most merchants over $25K/month, interchange-plus saves money. Under that, flat-rate often works out cleaner. We tell you which is right for you based on your statement, not a default sales pitch.

Are there monthly fees, statement fees, or PCI fees?

Possible standard fees: monthly account fee, monthly minimum (only if your processing is low enough to trigger it), and an annual PCI program fee. What you won't see from us: PCI non-compliance fees (we help you get and stay compliant), statement fees, mystery "regulatory" pass-throughs, or batch-close fees.

Every fee is labeled in plain English on your statement.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee for standard Clover configurations. For custom integrations (your POS we haven't worked with before, or unusual requirements), there may be a one-time engineering charge — quoted up-front, in writing, with a fixed scope. Never a surprise.

Are American Express, contactless, or premium cards extra?

Premium credit cards (rewards, business, international) carry higher interchange — that's the card network's pricing, not ours. We pass it through honestly on interchange-plus pricing. On flat-rate pricing, we blend it into your single rate.

Contactless (tap-to-pay, mobile wallets) is priced the same as chip — no surcharge.

Can you raise my rates whenever you want?

If card network costs change (Visa or Mastercard adjust interchange), we pass that through — we don't absorb increases. We give you written notice of any change to our markup with at least 30 days advance notice. If you don't like the change, you can leave penalty-free.

Will you really show me a better rate than Square?

For most merchants over ~$25K/month, yes — Square's flat rate (~2.65–2.9%) is hard to beat for tiny volume but expensive at scale. Below that threshold, sometimes Square's all-in tooling wins. We tell you honestly when staying on Square is the right call.

Can I get an itemized statement that shows every fee?

Yes. Every CleverPays statement is line-itemized by transaction, by card type, by fee. If anything on your statement is unclear, your account specialist will walk you through it line-by-line.

Contracts & cancellation

No three-year auto-renew. No hidden penalties. Here's exactly how our terms work.

Am I locked into a long contract?

Our processing is month-to-month. No multi-year auto-renewals. Hardware financing (if you choose to finance) is a separate agreement with its own term — typically 12, 24, or 36 months — and you own the device at the end. We'll send you the full terms before you sign anything.

If I cancel, is there an early termination fee?

No early termination fee on processing. If you bought hardware outright, it's yours. If you financed hardware, you complete the financing terms or pay it off early (no penalty). If you leased, the lease term applies — we'll always explain this in plain language up front.

What's the cancellation notice period?

30 days written notice. That's it. We don't add 60-day or 90-day "wind-down" clauses.

Can I see the contract before I commit?

Yes — and you should. We send you the full agreement before you sign, with all rates, fees, and terms in plain language. Take it to your accountant. We're not in a rush.

What if I try it for 30 days and want out?

Give us 30 days notice. We process your final batch, close your account, and that's it. If you bought hardware, you keep it (and can resell it). No drama, no retention department.

Hardware

Buy, finance, or lease. What it actually costs. What happens when something breaks.

How much does Clover hardware actually cost?

Approximate retail (purchase outright, before any financing): Mini ~$799 · Flex ~$599 · Station Solo ~$1,349 · Station Duo ~$1,649 · Kiosk varies by configuration. Bundle pricing applies for multi-device orders. Final pricing depends on accessories (cash drawer, kitchen printer, scale) and quantity.

For a typical 2-location restaurant: realistic full hardware bill ranges from $3K–$8K depending on configuration. We'll size it precisely for you.

Lease vs buy — what's the math?

Buying outright = lowest 3-year total cost. Financing through us spreads the cost over 12, 24, or 36 months with you owning the device at the end. Leasing (which we don't push) generally costs more over 3 years but reduces upfront cash.

Our team will model both for your specific configuration — usually that ends the debate.

What if a device breaks on a Saturday night?

For Saturday-night failures: our support line is staffed (EN/FR) every evening including weekends. We'll attempt remote diagnosis. If hardware replacement is needed, we ship next business day via expedited courier — and if you're within driving distance of Laval, we can sometimes hand-deliver a swap.

If you operate a high-volume location, we recommend keeping one backup device on-site — we can include this in the original quote.

Is there a warranty?

Standard Clover devices come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. We extend that with an in-house replacement policy: if a device fails within 3 years from normal use, we replace it. Damage caused by drops, liquids, or misuse is out of scope (insurance is your call).

Do you charge for shipping?

Standard shipping (3–5 business days) is included. Expedited shipping (next-day) for emergency replacements is included for active customers. New-order rush shipping carries a small pass-through fee.

What about cash drawer, printers, scanner, scale — extras?

Peripherals are quoted separately because they vary widely. Approximate retail: cash drawer ~$140, receipt printer (counter) ~$200, kitchen printer ~$400, barcode scanner ~$200, scale (grocery) varies $400–$2K. We bundle them into your full quote with no markup over our cost.

Can I reuse my Square hardware?

No — Square hardware is locked to Square processing. You can keep them as backup or resell. We don't run gimmicks where you can use Square Stand with CleverPays; that doesn't exist.

Switching from Square

You don't lose your business when you switch. Here's exactly what happens.

What stays the same when I switch?

Your customers (they don't notice anything except a different-looking receipt), your Square Online site if you have one (we replace only the payment processor underneath), your menu data (we export it from Square and import into Clover), and your transaction history (exported to CSV/PDF for your records and accountant).

What changes?

Hardware (Square hardware is locked to Square; you'll move to Clover). Your daily dashboard (Clover's dashboard replaces Square's). Settlement timing (often faster — same-day or next-business-day standard). Support model (named human vs ticket queue).

Will you do the menu migration for me, or do I have to re-enter every item?

We do the migration. You export your menu from Square (Items → Library → Export), send it to us, and our team imports it into Clover with modifiers, categories, and pricing intact. Most restaurants are running on Clover within a week of saying yes.

What about my Square gift cards already in circulation?

Outstanding Square gift cards stay redeemable through Square's system for a transition period. We can either let them age out organically or help you migrate the outstanding balance to a Clover gift program (this involves a one-time data export).

Can I run both Square and Clover during transition?

Yes — this is what we recommend for active restaurants. We typically keep Square live for the first 1–2 weeks while you and your staff get comfortable with Clover. Once you're settled, you cancel Square. No double-charging — each system processes its own sales.

Does Square charge me anything to leave?

No. Square is month-to-month with no termination fee. You just stop using them. (If you have an active Square Capital loan, that continues until paid off — independent of switching processors.)

Support & service

Real humans, in our Laval office, in your language. Here's exactly how it works.

What are your support hours?

Phone support: Monday–Friday 7am–11pm, Saturday–Sunday 9am–11pm Eastern. After-hours emergency line (hardware failure mid-service, settlement issue) is available 24/7. We staff Saturday and Sunday evenings because we know that's when restaurants need us.

Is support in French overnight and on weekends?

French support is available during all phone hours (7am–11pm weekdays, 9am–11pm weekends). The 24/7 emergency line is EN-only after midnight ET; we'll add FR overnight coverage as our team grows. You won't be routed to an offshore call center either way.

Will I have one named contact, or a ticket queue?

Both, in a good way. Every account gets a named account specialist — the person who set you up, who knows your stack, who calls you back. For after-hours or your specialist's day off, the support team handles it with full visibility into your account.

What's your average resolution time?

Phone answered: under 2 minutes (this is measured). First-call resolution rate: above 80% (most issues fixed on the same call). Hardware replacement when needed: next business day.

What if your team is unreachable for some reason?

For settlement or processing issues, you have direct fallback to the underlying processor and acquiring bank — they can intervene if we somehow couldn't. We provide those numbers and account references in your onboarding package. Belt-and-suspenders.

Will I get the same person 6 months from now?

Our team is stable. Specialists do change roles or leave occasionally — when they do, we introduce you to your new specialist personally, with a handover that includes everything we know about your business. No "starting from scratch" with a new rep.

Integrations

Your existing stack stays. We connect Clover to it.

Does Clover talk to QuickBooks?

Yes. Native Clover-to-QuickBooks Online integration is available through Clover's App Market. We configure it for you during setup. QuickBooks Desktop and Sage 50/Sage 100 integrations are available via third-party connectors (Commerce Sync, MyWorks) — we recommend the right one for your accountant.

Does it work with Uber Eats, DoorDash, Skip the Dishes?

Yes — Clover has direct integrations or trusted middleware for all three. Orders flow into your Clover kitchen display, prices stay in sync, and end-of-day reports consolidate delivery orders with dine-in. We set this up during onboarding.

Online Ordering — is that really free?

Yes — Clover's online ordering is included free at the software tier appropriate for your business. For full-service restaurants, that's the Table Service plan. For QSR, it's the Counter Service plan. There's no extra fee per online order beyond the standard card processing rate.

We'll tell you precisely which Clover plan fits your operation (and what it costs) during the quote — never as a surprise.

What if I want to keep my current online ordering provider?

Workable. We can integrate most third-party ordering platforms (ChowNow, Toast Online, custom builds) with Clover via middleware or direct API. We tell you up-front what's possible and what isn't.

Does it integrate with my payroll provider?

Most major payroll systems (Wagepoint, Push Operations, 7shifts) have native or middleware integration with Clover for hours, sales, and tip reporting. We confirm during setup.

Security & PCI

What you're protected from. What you're liable for. In plain language.

What's PCI compliance and am I covered?

PCI-DSS is the card industry's security standard. Merchants must comply. We help you stay compliant through Clover's built-in tools and an annual self-assessment we walk you through. There's no "PCI non-compliance" punishment fee from us if you fall behind — we get you compliant instead.

If a customer's card data gets stolen, who pays?

If the breach is on the Clover device or our infrastructure — that's our problem to resolve. If the breach is on your end (employee fraud, your network, your physical security), you may be liable. PCI compliance and good practices substantially reduce both risks.

Where is customer card data stored?

Card data never lands on your POS or your business network. It's encrypted at the terminal and transmitted directly to the processor. We store tokenized references only — never the actual card numbers.

Are you EMV, contactless, and chip-secure?

Yes — every Clover device supports EMV chip, contactless (tap-to-pay), magstripe, and mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay). PCI PTS certified hardware.

Settlement & money

When you get paid. What can delay it. No surprises.

When does the money hit my account?

Standard settlement: next business day for most card types. Same-day settlement available for established accounts. Weekends and holidays delay deposits to the next business day (standard for the entire industry).

Is there a rolling reserve or hold on my new account?

For most established small businesses: no reserve. For high-risk verticals, very new businesses with no operating history, or businesses with very large average tickets, a small reserve may apply temporarily. We tell you up-front during underwriting if any reserve applies.

Can you freeze my funds?

Funds can be held if there's unusual activity (sudden 10x volume spike, chargebacks running above threshold, suspected fraud). When this happens, we call you immediately — we don't disappear with your money. Most "freezes" resolve within 24–48 hours once we verify what's happening.

Do I have to switch my bank?

No. We deposit into whatever Canadian business bank account you already have. RBC, TD, BMO, Scotia, CIBC, National, Desjardins, credit unions — all standard.

What about refund fees?

When you refund a customer, the original interchange and assessment portion of the fee is generally returned to you. Our processor markup on the original sale is not. We don't charge an additional refund fee.

Multi-location

Two locations or twenty — consolidated reporting, per-location flexibility.

Can I see consolidated reporting across all my locations?

Yes. Clover's multi-location dashboard rolls up sales, labor, inventory, and tips across every location. You can drill into a single location or view the rollup. Real-time on web and mobile.

Can I have different menus or prices per location?

Yes. Each location can have its own menu, item availability, prices, taxes, and operating hours. Items shared across locations sync automatically — items unique to a location stay there.

Do you charge per location?

Per-location software costs apply (each location runs its own Clover software plan). Processing rates are negotiated based on total volume across all locations — meaning more locations usually means better rates.

What if I grow from 2 to 5 locations?

Easy. We add the new locations under your existing account, deploy hardware, configure menus and staff, and re-negotiate processing rates downward based on your new volume. Growth shouldn't punish you.

Quebec specifics

Quebec has unique requirements. We know them cold.

Does this comply with Revenu Québec MEV / SRM requirements for restaurants?

Yes. Quebec restaurants are required to use a Sales Recording Module (MEV / SRM) for invoicing. Clover devices we sell to Quebec restaurants are configured with the certified SRM integration — we handle the connection, certification paperwork, and ongoing compliance.

Does it handle GST/QST correctly?

Yes — both taxes are configured natively, applied at the correct rates per item, and reported correctly to your accountant. We configure tax-exempt customer flags too (for non-profits, schools, certain healthcare).

Can I get bilingual receipts for customers?

Yes. Receipts can be EN, FR, or printed bilingually. Configurable per-location.

Will the Clover devices and software be fully in French for my staff?

Yes. Clover supports French interface fully. We configure devices in French (or per-employee language) before shipping. Training materials, support documentation, and quick-reference cards are available in French.
