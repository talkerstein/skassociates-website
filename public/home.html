Pricing & fees

The most-asked questions. Rates depend on your card mix and channel — but here's exactly how pricing works.

What's my actual rate going to be?

Effective rate depends on your card mix (debit vs credit vs premium), average ticket, monthly volume, and channel. For a typical small restaurant or retail merchant, blended effective rates land between 1.9% and 2.6%. The only way to give a real number is to read your statement — we do that in 24 hours.

Is it flat-rate or interchange-plus?

We offer both. Interchange-plus shows the actual card-network cost separately from our markup. Flat-rate is one number, no math. Above ~$25K/month, interchange-plus usually saves money. Under that, flat-rate often wins cleaner.

Are there monthly fees, statement fees, or PCI fees?

Possible standard fees: monthly account fee, monthly minimum (only if volume triggers it), annual PCI program fee. What you won't see: PCI non-compliance fees, statement fees, mystery regulatory pass-throughs, batch-close fees. Every fee labeled in plain English.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee for standard Clover configurations. Custom integrations may carry a one-time engineering charge — quoted up-front, in writing, fixed scope.

Are Amex, contactless, or premium cards extra?

Premium credit cards carry higher interchange — card-network pricing, not ours. Pass-through on interchange-plus, blended on flat-rate. Contactless (tap, mobile wallets) priced the same as chip — no surcharge.

Can you raise my rates whenever you want?

Card-network cost changes pass through. Our markup changes only with 30 days written notice. If you don't like a change, you can leave penalty-free.

Will you really beat my Square rate?

For most merchants over ~$25K/month, yes — Square's flat rate is expensive at scale. Below that, sometimes Square wins. We tell you honestly when staying on Square is the right call.

Itemized statement?

Yes. Every statement is line-itemized by transaction, by card type, by fee. If anything is unclear, your specialist walks you through it.

Processing is month-to-month. No multi-year auto-renewals. Hardware financing is separate, with its own term — you own the device at the end.

None on processing. Hardware financing/lease has its own terms (explained plainly up-front).

30 days written notice. No 60- or 90-day wind-downs.

See the contract before signing?

Yes. Full agreement before signing, with all rates, fees, and terms in plain language. Take it to your accountant.

30-day trial out?

Give us 30 days notice. We close your account, you keep your hardware. No retention department.

Hardware

Buy, finance, or lease. What it actually costs.

How much does Clover hardware cost?

Approximate retail: Mini ~$799 · Flex ~$599 · Station Solo ~$1,349 · Station Duo ~$1,649 · Kiosk varies. Bundle discounts apply for multi-device orders.

Lease vs buy — what's better?

Buying outright wins on 3-year total cost. Financing through us spreads cost over 12/24/36 months — you own at the end. We don't push leasing.

What if a device breaks Saturday night?

Support staffed (EN/FR) every evening including weekends. Remote diagnosis first; hardware swap ships next business day. We recommend a backup on-site for high-volume locations.

Warranty?

1-year manufacturer warranty + our in-house replacement policy for 3 years of normal-use failures. Drops, liquids, misuse out of scope.

Shipping charges?

Standard shipping included. Expedited replacements for active customers — included. Rush shipping on new orders carries a small pass-through fee.

Peripherals — extras?

Quoted separately. Approximate: cash drawer ~$140, receipt printer ~$200, kitchen printer ~$400, scanner ~$200. Bundled into your quote with no markup.

Reuse my Square hardware?

No — Square hardware is locked to Square. Keep as backup or resell.

Switching from Square

Exactly what stays, what changes, how long.

What stays the same?

Your customers (different-looking receipt), your Square Online site (we replace only the processor), your menu data (we migrate it), your transaction history (CSV/PDF).

What changes?

Hardware. Daily dashboard. Settlement timing (usually faster). Support model (named human, not ticket queue).

Menu migration — manual?

We do it. You export from Square, send to us. Our team imports into Clover with modifiers, categories, pricing intact.

Outstanding Square gift cards?

Stay redeemable through Square during transition. We can migrate outstanding balances to a Clover gift program.

Run Square + Clover in parallel?

Yes — we recommend this for active restaurants. Square stays live 1–2 weeks while staff get comfortable. Then cancel Square.

Does Square charge me to leave?

No. Square is month-to-month with no termination fee.

Security & PCI

What you're protected from. What you're liable for.

PCI compliance — am I covered?

We help you stay compliant through Clover's built-in tools and an annual self-assessment we walk you through. No "PCI non-compliance" punishment fee.

Card data stolen — who pays?

Clover device or our infrastructure breach: our problem. Your-side breach (employee fraud, your network): you may be liable. PCI compliance reduces both.

Where is card data stored?

Never on your POS or business network. Encrypted at the terminal, transmitted directly to the processor. Tokenized references only.

EMV, contactless, chip-secure?

Every Clover: EMV chip, contactless (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay), magstripe. PCI PTS certified.

Settlement & money

When you get paid. What can delay it.

When does money hit my account?

Standard: next business day. Same-day for established accounts. Weekends/holidays delay to next business day.

Rolling reserve on a new account?

Most established small businesses: no reserve. High-risk verticals or very-new businesses: small temporary reserve may apply. Disclosed up-front.

Can you freeze my funds?

Only on unusual activity (10x volume spike, chargebacks over threshold, suspected fraud). We call you immediately. Most resolve in 24–48 hours.

Switch banks?

No. RBC, TD, BMO, Scotia, CIBC, National, Desjardins, credit unions — all standard.

Refund fees?

Original interchange/assessment is returned to you. Our processor markup on the original sale isn't. No additional refund fee from us.
