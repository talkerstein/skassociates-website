Real money, real talk

What does this actually cost?

Two things drive your cost: hardware (one-time or financed) and processing (per-transaction, ongoing). We don't post a single rate sheet because your real rate depends on your card mix, average ticket, and channel — but we explain every fee that goes into your number, and we tell you which fees you simply won't see from us.

✓What your processing fee covers
  • Interchange — cost paid to Visa/Mastercard/Amex/Interac. Set by card networks, not us.
  • Assessments — small network fees. Pass-through.
  • Processor markup — our slice. Spelled out plainly on every quote.
  • Settlement & funding — daily deposit into your bank.
  • EMV chip, tap, mobile wallet acceptance — same price as a swipe.
  • Tokenization & PCI vault — secure storage of card data.
  • Bilingual support & in-house integration — every call, every escalation, included.
✕What you won't see on a CleverPays statement
  • PCI non-compliance fees — we help you stay compliant, not punish you.
  • Statement fees — your monthly statement is free.
  • Mystery "regulatory" pass-throughs — clear names or no pass.
  • Batch close fees — closing your day shouldn't cost extra.
  • Refund processing fees — refunding doesn't carry its own charge.
  • Three-year auto-renew traps — month-to-month means month-to-month.
  • Early-termination fees — leave with 30 days notice.

Coming from Square (or anyone)?

The switch, honestly explained.

You've heard processors promise easy switches before. Here's what actually happens — every step, every cost, every risk — so you can decide with eyes open.

What stays the same

Your customers (only see a different-looking receipt). Your menu (we migrate it from Square). Your transaction history (exported to CSV/PDF). Your eCommerce site. Your bank account.

What changes

Hardware (Square hardware only runs Square — you'll move to Clover). The daily dashboard. Settlement timing (usually faster). Support model (named human instead of ticket queue).

How long it takes

Single location: 1–2 weeks. Two locations: 2–3 weeks. Multi-location franchises: 4–6 weeks, staged by location. We work around your peak hours — never your dinner service.

Week-by-week, what actually happens:

01

Discovery (day 0–3)

Send us your last Square statement and a few setup photos. We come back in 24 hours with a precise quote, hardware recommendation, and integration plan.

02

Provision (day 4–7)

Hardware is configured for your menu, tax setup, employee permissions, and peripherals — before it leaves Laval. Your account is underwritten and activated.

03

Migrate (day 7–10)

We import your Square menu into Clover. Set up online ordering, delivery integrations, accounting connection. Train your staff (on-site if you're nearby).

04

Go live (day 10–14)

Clover live in parallel with Square for a week — zero risk. Once you're comfortable, you cancel Square. We monitor your first 7 days actively.

When something breaks

Saturday night, mid-service. What happens.

Things break. Internet drops. Devices stop responding. Settlement disputes happen. Here's exactly what we do — and what you do — in the four scenarios merchants ask about most.

Hardware failure

"My terminal won't turn on. It's Saturday at 7pm."

Call our support line — staffed every evening including weekends, EN/FR. We attempt remote diagnosis first (most "broken" devices are software hiccups we fix in 5 minutes). If hardware truly failed: replacement ships next business day. Within driving distance of Laval, we sometimes hand-deliver. We recommend a backup device for high-volume locations.

Internet down

"The internet just died. We're full."

Clover Flex has cellular backup built-in — keeps processing on 4G LTE. Counter Clovers (Mini, Station) work in offline mode for card payments; transactions queue locally and sync when internet returns. We configure offline mode during onboarding so it Just Works.

Settlement holdup

"My daily deposit didn't show up."

Usually bank-side (your bank's processing time) or trigger-side (unusual activity flagged by underwriter). Call us — we have direct lines to the processor and acquiring bank. Most settlement holds resolve in 24–48 hours.

Chargeback dispute

"A customer disputed a charge."

You'll get a notification with dispute details and a deadline. We walk you through what evidence to submit (receipt, signature/PIN proof, refund policy). Our team helps build the response. For repeat disputes from the same customer, we escalate.

Pricing & fees

The most-asked questions.

What's my actual rate?

Depends on card mix, ticket size, monthly volume, channel. Typical small restaurant/retail blend: 1.9% to 2.6%. We give a precise number after reading your statement (24h turnaround).

Flat-rate or interchange-plus?

Both available. Interchange-plus = transparent (network cost separate from our markup). Flat-rate = simple. Over ~$25K/month, interchange-plus usually saves money.

Monthly fees, statement fees, PCI fees?

Possible: monthly account fee, monthly minimum (only if volume triggers it), annual PCI program fee. Never: PCI non-compliance fees, statement fees, mystery pass-throughs, batch-close fees.

Setup fee?

None for standard Clover configurations. Custom integrations: one-time engineering charge if applicable, quoted up-front.

Amex, contactless, premium cards extra?

Premium cards carry higher interchange (network pricing). Passed through honestly on interchange-plus, blended on flat-rate. Contactless = same as chip.

Can you raise rates whenever?

Card-network changes pass through. Our markup changes only with 30 days written notice. Don't like it? Leave penalty-free.

Beat Square's rate?

Over ~$25K/month: yes, usually. Below: sometimes Square wins. We tell you honestly.

Itemized statement?

Yes. Line-itemized by transaction, card type, fee. Anything unclear? Your specialist walks you through.
