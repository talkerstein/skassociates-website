Common questions

Things merchants ask us most.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. Our Clover processing is month-to-month. We don't believe in three-year auto-renew traps. If our service isn't earning your business, you should be able to leave — and you can.

How fast can a Clover be set up?

Most merchants are accepting payments within a few business days of saying yes. Hardware is in stock, configured before it ships, and ready to plug in. Integration into accounting or inventory systems is usually completed within a week.

Can I keep my current POS and just use Clover for payments?

Yes — that's exactly what Clover Integrated is for, and it's one of our most-requested setups. Learn more about Integrated POS.

What does "Clover Authorized Dealer" mean for me?

It means we have direct authority to provision Clover devices, configure your merchant account, push software updates, troubleshoot deeply, and escalate to Clover's engineering team when needed. We're not a reseller routing your call to someone else.

Is support actually in-house and bilingual?

Yes. Our support team is in our Laval office. EN or FR, first language. Average answer time is under two minutes on our support line. The agent you speak with sits beside the engineer who can fix integration issues.

What if I outgrow my first Clover?

You scale within the ecosystem. A merchant who starts with a Mini can add a Flex, expand to a Station Duo, deploy Kiosks, or add Integrated terminals — all on the same Clover account, same software, same reporting. We help plan and execute every upgrade.

Do you handle multi-location setups?

Yes — Clover scales cleanly across locations with consolidated reporting and per-location permissions. We've deployed everything from two-location operators to multi-province franchises.
