Decision support

How to choose between them.

A few honest comparisons. We'd rather you make the right call than the fastest one.

Choose Clover when…

You want a full POS ecosystem (devices + software + apps + reporting) under one roof, with native online ordering, loyalty, and inventory. Best for restaurants, retail, and merchants who want everything to "just work" together.

Choose Ingenico Axium when…

You have a strong POS already (or strong integration partner) and want a premium customer-facing terminal with modern UX. The Axium DX8000/EX8000 shines when the terminal is part of the experience — high-end retail, branded environments.

Choose Pax A920 when…

You need a mobile, all-day, smart handheld at a strong price point. Common for service businesses, mobile retail, food trucks, market vendors, and merchants who want capability without complexity.

Choose Verifone V400m / T650 when…

You need maximum security, demanding-environment reliability, or you're integrating into a POS system that's already on Verifone's Engage platform via Eigen. Common in healthcare, multi-location retail, and high-traffic hospitality.

Common questions

Smart terminal FAQ.

If you're a Clover Authorized Dealer, why offer alternatives?

Because we work for merchants, not vendors. Clover is excellent — but it's not the right fit for every business. When the right answer is Ingenico, Pax, or Verifone, we'd rather offer it than push a Clover that won't serve you well.

Do these terminals integrate with my existing POS?

Yes, in most cases — through middleware we configure and support. Most commonly: FLEX (semi-integrated), Datacap NETePay (PIN pad bridge), and Eigen MiraServ (Verifone Engage platform). See Integrated POS for details.

Is support the same as for Clover?

Yes. Our in-house bilingual support team handles every brand we sell. Same response times, same authority, same accountability.

Do these terminals support contactless and mobile wallets?

Yes — every terminal listed accepts tap-to-pay, NFC, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay. Plus chip insert, magstripe swipe, and all major card networks.

Can I mix brands across locations?

Yes — and many of our multi-location merchants do. We help you standardize where it makes sense and customize where each location's needs differ. Reporting consolidates across brands when your gateway supports it.
