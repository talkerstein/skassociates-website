Mini FAQ

Common Mini questions.

Is the Mini powerful enough for a busy cafe?

Yes — comfortably. The Mini handles hundreds of transactions a day, real-time inventory updates, and the full Clover software suite without slowing down. The 8" screen is generous for menu interaction.

Can I add a barcode scanner?

Yes. The Mini supports USB and Bluetooth barcode scanners. We include one in the quote if your operation needs it.

Does the Mini work for a salon with appointments?

Excellent for it. Pair with a Clover-compatible appointment app (Booker, Square Appointments integration, Mindbody) for full schedule + payment flow on one device.

Mini vs Flex — which one for a small cafe?

Mini if customers come to a fixed counter. Flex if you take orders from tables or roam the floor. Many cafes run both — Mini at the counter, Flex for catering or tableside service.

Can I print kitchen tickets from the Mini?

Yes — through a separate kitchen printer connected to the Clover network. The Mini's built-in printer handles customer receipts; kitchen tickets go to the printer in the back. Both are managed from the same dashboard.
