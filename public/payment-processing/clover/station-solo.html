Station Solo FAQ

Common Solo questions.

Solo vs Duo — what's the real difference?

Same hardware horsepower. Same software. The Duo has a dedicated 8" customer-facing display; the Solo doesn't. Pick Duo for restaurants, QSR, and customer-engaging retail (tips, loyalty prompts). Pick Solo for everywhere else.

Can I add a customer display later?

Yes — pair a Clover Mini with the Solo as a customer-facing add-on, or upgrade the whole setup. Your data and configuration carry over completely.

Is the Solo good for restaurants?

For takeout-only or counter-service places: yes. For full-service restaurants with tipping and customer engagement at the counter, the Duo usually pays for itself in higher tips alone.

Same peripherals as Duo?

Yes — cash drawer, receipt printer, kitchen printer, scanner, scale. Same ecosystem.
