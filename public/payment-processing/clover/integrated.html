Integrated FAQ

Common Integrated questions.

What's the difference between Clover Integrated and just buying a Clover Mini?

Mini is a standalone POS that runs Clover software. Integrated is a payment terminal that connects to your existing POS — you keep your POS interface, your menu management, your staff workflows. Card data doesn't touch your POS at all (better security + reduced PCI scope).

Will it work with my [POS]?

Most likely yes. We integrate with Lightspeed, Toast, TouchBistro, Aldelo, Aloha, Maitre'D, Square (migration), Talech, Revel, custom-built, and many vertical-specific systems. Ask us about yours — our engineering team has integrated with a lot more than fits on a list. See our full Integrated POS page.

What changes for my staff?

Almost nothing. They ring sales on your existing POS exactly as before. When payment is due, the POS sends the amount to the Clover terminal. Customer pays. Done. No relearning, no workflow change.

Who supports it if something breaks?

We do. End-to-end. If the issue turns out to be your POS vendor's responsibility, we coordinate the escalation on your behalf instead of handing you a phone number.

Does it work with kitchen display systems or printers?

Yes — your POS handles order routing to kitchen as it does today. The Clover terminal handles payment only. Cleaner separation, fewer points of failure.
