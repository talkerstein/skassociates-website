Flex FAQ

Common Flex questions.

Does the Flex work without Wi-Fi?

Yes — 4G LTE is built in. If Wi-Fi drops, the Flex keeps processing on cellular. We can ship the device with cellular pre-provisioned or use your existing carrier.

How long does the battery last?

~8 hours of typical use (a full restaurant shift, a market day, a service-call route). The dock charges between shifts. For very heavy use, we recommend keeping a charged spare.

Can I print bigger receipts or kitchen tickets from the Flex?

The built-in printer handles standard 2"-wide receipts. For kitchen tickets, the Flex sends orders to a separate kitchen printer (which we include in your setup if needed).

Does it sync with my Station Duo?

Yes — all Clover devices share one account, one menu, one inventory, one customer list, one reporting dashboard. The Flex talks to the Station in real time.

Can I use the Flex offline?

Yes — Clover Offline Payments queues transactions locally during brief connectivity drops and syncs when back online. We configure offline limits during setup.

Tips on the Flex?

Customer-facing tip prompts are configurable — fixed amounts, percentages, or custom. Tips flow to Clover reporting and into 7shifts / payroll integrations.
