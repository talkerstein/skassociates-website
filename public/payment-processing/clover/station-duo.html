Station Duo FAQ

Common Duo questions.

Do I need a Duo or a Solo?

Pick Duo if your customers benefit from seeing the cart (most retail and QSR — for accuracy, tips, and loyalty engagement). Pick Solo if the customer interaction is at-the-back-of-the-counter style where a customer display doesn't add value. Many merchants choose Duo for the dramatic tip uplift alone.

Will my staff need retraining moving from Square Stand or Register?

Most staff are productive in under an hour. The Clover UI is intentionally similar to other modern POS systems. We provide a 30-minute walkthrough at go-live, plus EN/FR quick-reference cards for the counter.

Can the Station Duo run offline?

Yes for short outages — Clover Offline Payments queues transactions locally and syncs when back online. We configure offline limits per merchant.

Does the Duo integrate with kitchen display systems?

Yes — Clover KDS, third-party KDS via Clover App Market, or a traditional kitchen printer. Orders route to the right station automatically based on item-category routing rules we configure.

Can I add a second customer-facing display elsewhere?

The Duo's customer display is dedicated and attached. For a drive-thru or remote customer screen, we recommend pairing with a Mini or a dedicated customer kiosk app.
